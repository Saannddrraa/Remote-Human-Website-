Primer - an early stage pre-accelerator startup program for the first time in Novi Sad

November 13, 2019, Novi Sad, Serbia

Primer by InnoEnergy is a pre-accelerator program organized under the partnership of Startit and InnoEnergy.

Primer by InnoEnergy is a full-day program that aims to introduce teams over six days with methods and tools that will help them progress further. Teams will have the opportunity to learn, through hands-on work, conversation and lectures, from mentors made up of successful entrepreneurs and experts in the various fields relevant to startups.

The program will be organized for the first time outside Belgrade at the Startit Center in Novi Sad.

During lectures and intensive work with mentors, teams will learn, among other things, how to create a business model canvas, manage finances and get investments, legal aspects of running a startup, go-to-market and sales strategies, pitching and pitch deck structure.
