Brokerage Event - the second phase of the DIH² project

DECEMBER 16, 2019 10:00 AM, Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Serbia, Belgrade

The meeting is held on the occasion of the opening of the second phase of the first open call for DIH² - A Pan-European Network of Robotics DIHs for Agile Production.

The first phase of application for the DIH² project has been successfully completed, and from December 3, 2019, the second phase of the open call began until February 27, 2020 at 1:00 pm. Unlike the first phase in which individual firms applied, in the second phase, consortia consisting of one Manufacturing SME and 1-2 technology providers (Tech Provider) apply.

The aim of the meeting, on the one hand, is for the companies present to get to know each other better, to present their ideas for the project, and to pair the firms where possible. On the other hand, the goal of the meeting is to present the application process for the second phase and go through the application forms.
