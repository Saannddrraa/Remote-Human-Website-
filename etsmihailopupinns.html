SMART BOBBY AT ELECTROTECHNICAL SCHOOL MIHAILO PUPIN Novi Sad, Serbia

November 20, 2019

ELECTROTECHNICAL SCHOOL Mihailo Pupin was established by decision of the People's Committee of Novi Sad Municipality, May 16, 1963. The need for the establishment of such a school institution arose from the development of the economy of Vojvodina in the sixties, which required a greater number of personnel of the electrical profession than the one who had by that time been educated at the technical school "Bosko Palkovljevic Pinky", a school for highly skilled workers, an industrial school with a practical training and at the school of students in the economy "Svetozar Miletic".

Remote Human team has a presentation of Smart Bobby at Electrotechnical school Mihailo Pupin in front of more than 200 students! Everyone wanted to see how our Smart Bobby works.
